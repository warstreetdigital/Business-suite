HOW THIS BUSINESS WORKS

You operate a laundry services business without owning machines, staff, or premises. This store exists to coordinate demand, confirm payments, and control flow. You are the single authority point between customers, suppliers, and courier.

SERVICES OFFERED

Wash & Dry · Wash, Dry & Fold · Ironing · Dry Cleaning · Student & Family Laundry · Small Business Laundry

PAYMENT LOGIC

50% upfront and 50% on completion. Accepted methods are EcoCash and Cash. No work begins before payment confirmation. Courier fees are separate.

SUPPLIERS & QUALITY

Suppliers handle cleaning only. They do not handle payments or customer communication. You assign suppliers and control flow.

ORDER FLOW

Inquiry → Confirmation → Payment → Supplier → Courier → Completion → Close

DISPUTES & ESCALATION

Confirm facts first. Communicate clearly. Escalate only if a supplier defaults. Do not refund money you did not receive.
